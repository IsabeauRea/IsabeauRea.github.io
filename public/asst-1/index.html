Birds With Arms Gym Routine

(Routine for Birds Trying to Get Swole)

  1. Lifted Wing Lifts

    • Adhere Bird-arms to the ground in a fist formation
    • Lift Bird-legs off the ground
    • 15 Wing Lifts

  2. Stretches

    • 15 Beak Stretches
    • 10 Talon Lifts
    • 20 Tailfeather Ruffles

  3. Manual Wing Lifts

    • Fly using your Bird-arms to flap your wings
    • This should be attempted above WATER
    • We do not accept responsibility for injuries sustained during activities.
